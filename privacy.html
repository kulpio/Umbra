Privacy

Last updated: 2026-07-19 · Honest minimum notice for a waitlist. Not legal advice.

Who we are

Umbra is a working product codename for a planned tool that helps people see and control permissions: OAuth and connected access, AI agent grants, and location access — with inbox archaeology as a method later (client-side where possible), not a subscription-canceler product.

Founder contact (interim): d.demnard@gmail.com — questions, waitlist issues, and deletion requests.

Postal address will be published before we send any mail.

What we collect

  • Email address — when you join the waitlist.
  • Optional preference — permissions / agents / location / all (if you choose one).
  • Angle / source tags — which pitch you saw (e.g. access, agents, location) and basic referrer/UTM if present, to improve the message. Not a surveillance profile.
  • Basic server/logs — standard host logs (IP, user-agent, path) may be retained briefly by the host (e.g. GitHub Pages). We do not run a custom tracking stack on this site by default.
  • Form delivery — waitlist submissions are delivered via FormSubmit to the founder inbox so we can store the list offline. See FormSubmit’s own privacy terms for their processing.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not share waitlist emails with Meta/Google for lookalike audiences or ads.
  • We do not load ad or retargeting pixels on this site.
  • We do not scan your inbox from this page. There is no Gmail connect here.

Why we collect it

Waitlist and product updates about Umbra early access only. We may email you when the private alpha opens or with rare product news. Unsubscribe will be available on every email once sending starts.

Global Privacy Control (GPC)

If your browser sends the Global Privacy Control signal (Sec-GPC: 1 or navigator.globalPrivacyControl), we treat that as a do-not-sell / do-not-share preference. This static site does not load non-essential third-party trackers by default. Any future optional analytics will respect GPC and will not load when GPC is set.

Retention & deletion

We keep waitlist emails until you unsubscribe or ask to be removed, or until the project ends. To request deletion, email d.demnard@gmail.com (founder contact, interim) with the address to remove, or use unsubscribe links in any email we send once mailing starts.

Children

This waitlist is not directed at children under 13. Do not submit their emails.

Changes

We may update this notice. The date above will change when we do.
